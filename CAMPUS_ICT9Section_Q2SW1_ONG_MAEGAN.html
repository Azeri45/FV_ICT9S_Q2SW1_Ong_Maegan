Corgi
  • The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a muscular, athletic, and lively little herder who is affectionate and companionable without being needy, making it one of the most agreeable small house dogs. They are one of the most popular herding breeds in the world.

Bichon Frise
  • The small but sturdy and resilient Bichon Frise is one of the world's great 'personality dogs,' relying on charm, beauty, and intelligence to weather history's ups and downs since antiquity.
